Browser-side text generation

This tool is designed to generate your Discord ANSI output in your browser. We do not intentionally store the message text you type into the generator on our servers in the MVP. Standard website requests and hosting logs may still exist. Do not enter sensitive, confidential, or private information.

Website requests and logs

When you visit the site, hosting or CDN systems may process technical request data such as IP address, user agent, URL, timestamp, referrer, and security/debug logs to deliver the site and keep it reliable.

Analytics and cookies

No optional analytics, advertising, affiliate tracking, account system, AI provider, payment provider, upload storage, or database is enabled in this MVP implementation. If analytics is added later, events must be aggregate only and must not include raw user text, selected spans, generated ANSI output, clipboard content, usernames, server names, or message content.
